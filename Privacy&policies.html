Gym Club Policies

Last updated: March 2026

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Blessing's Gym Club collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our services or visit our website.

Information We Collect

  • Full name, phone number, email address
  • Membership details and payment records
  • Emergency contact information
  • Check-in logs for gym access
  • CCTV footage for safety and security
  • Health and fitness information you voluntarily provide to trainers

How We Use Your Information

  • To manage your membership and payments
  • To provide training, fitness programs, and class bookings
  • To maintain safety and security in the gym
  • To communicate important updates and schedule changes
  • To comply with legal and safety requirements

Sharing Your Information

We do NOT sell personal information. We may share limited information with:

  • Payment processors for billing
  • Security services (CCTV monitoring if applicable)
  • Authorized staff and fitness instructors

Member Rights

  • Request access to your personal information
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion (where legally allowed)

Terms & Conditions

By joining [Blessing's Gym Club], you agree to follow the policies and rules below.

Gym Membership

  • Membership fees must be paid on time.
  • Membership is non-transferable unless approved by management.
  • ID or membership card may be required on entry.

Health & Safety

  • Members must notify staff of any medical conditions.
  • Proper gym attire and shoes must be worn at all times.
  • Equipment must be used safely and returned after use.
  • No misuse of weights or machines.

Code of Conduct

  • Respect trainers, staff, and other members.
  • No harassment, bullying, or inappropriate behavior.
  • No smoking, alcohol, or illegal substances in the gym.
  • Clean equipment after use.

Cancellation

  • Membership cancellation requires written notice.
  • Cancellations are subject to the gym’s cancellation policy.

Cookie Policy (Website)

Our website uses cookies to improve your experience.

Types of Cookies

  • Essential – required for login and booking
  • Analytics – helps us improve the website
  • Preferences – remembers your settings

Managing Cookies

You can disable cookies in your browser, but some features may not work.

Refund & Cancellation Policy

Membership Refunds

  • Refunds are only issued within the first 7 days of membership.
  • After 7 days, membership fees are non-refundable.

Class or Session Refunds

  • Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours before the class.
  • Late cancellations or no-shows are not refundable.

Personal Training Packages

  • Unused sessions expire after the period stated in the package.
  • Sessions are non-transferable and non-refundable unless required by law.
